Muhammad Ali

One of the most famouse atheletes in sports history

Muhammad Ali was a former American professional boxer, nicknamed the greatest, and considered as the greatestheavyweight in sports history.
He was known for his skills inside the ring and values he exemplified outside the ring.
He was an image for religion, racial struggles, police injustice, and much more.
