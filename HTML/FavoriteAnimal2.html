Cheetah Feeding Habits

The Cheetah is a carnivore. Its diet consists of animals such as gazelles, wildebeest calves, impalas, and smaller hoofed animals in its habitat. The Cheetah would alsoeat small rabbits, birds and other smallcreature too.
